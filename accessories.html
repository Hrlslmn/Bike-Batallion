Helmetlok

Helmetlok allows riders to secure their helmets to their bikes when they are parked. The Helmetlok device is a small carabiner-style lock that attaches to the bike's frame or handlebars using a T-Bar mechanism. The rider can then thread the lock through the D-rings or chin strap of their helmet, securing it to the bike.

The Helmetlok is designed to be easy to use and portable, so riders can carry it with them on their bike or in their pocket when they are out and about. This makes it a convenient solution for riders who want to keep their helmet secure when they are off their bike, such as when they are running errands or grabbing a bite to eat.

Tire Pressure Monitor

A tire pressure monitor, also known as a TPMS, is a system that is designed to monitor the air pressure in a vehicle's tires. The TPMS system uses sensors mounted inside the tires or on the valve stems to monitor the air pressure and send this information to a control unit in the vehicle.

The control unit then uses this information to determine if the tire pressure is too low, too high, or within the recommended range. If the pressure is outside the recommended range, the system will typically alert the driver with a warning light or message on the dashboard.

Having properly inflated tires is important for several reasons. Tires that are underinflated can lead to reduced fuel efficiency, uneven wear on the tires, and a decreased handling and braking performance. Overinflated tires can also lead to decreased handling performance and increased wear on the tires.

Scottoiler esystem electronic moto chain oiler

The Scottoiler eSystem is a motorcycle chain lubrication system that is designed to automatically apply chain lubricant to the motorcycle chain while the bike is in motion. The eSystem consists of a reservoir that contains the chain lubricant, an electronic control unit, and a pump that delivers the lubricant to the chain.

The eSystem uses sensors to detect when the motorcycle is in motion, and then applies the lubricant to the chain at a rate that is determined by the speed of the bike. This helps to ensure that the chain is properly lubricated at all times, which can help to extend its lifespan and improve its performance.

he eSystem is easy to install and can be customized to suit the specific needs of the rider. The control unit allows the rider to adjust the rate of lubricant delivery and to set up a maintenance schedule based on the rider's preferences. The eSystem is also designed to be energy-efficient, with a low power consumption that helps to minimize its impact on the motorcycle's battery.
